I Made A Name List!

My job routinely requires me to name certain things (ie. ships for simulation) and thus I made a list of 100 names in 10 categories to pull from. The list includes the names or surnames of real and fictional people (and a dog, and two objects). I have uploaded the file to the website, you can find it under the 'files' page, feel free to use it for whatever purpose.

I may grow the list further in the future, in which case the file will be updated. If it's a particularly big update, I might make another blog post about it as well.
